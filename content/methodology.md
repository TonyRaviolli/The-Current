# Methodology

The UnderCurrent ranks stories using transparent signals: source tier quality, recency decay, topic boosts, local relevance, and duplication penalties. We favor official feeds and wire services, avoid scraping full articles, and never ingest raw HTML into the UI.

## What counts as "high importance"

A story qualifies when it exceeds a score threshold and matches priority topics like macroeconomics, geopolitics, defense, or critical technology regulation.

## Source tiers

- Tier 1: Global wire services and primary outlets.
- Tier 2: Reputable analysis and trade publications.
- Tier 3: Local/regional outlets aligned to the configured region.
