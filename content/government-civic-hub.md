## Government & Civic Intelligence Hub

### U.S. Federal Core
- [Congress.gov](https://www.congress.gov/) - bills, hearings, and voting records.
- [White House Briefing Room](https://www.whitehouse.gov/briefing-room/) - executive orders and official statements.
- [Federal Register](https://www.federalregister.gov/) - daily regulatory actions.
- [Office of Management and Budget](https://www.whitehouse.gov/omb/) - federal budget and policy memoranda.
- [U.S. Treasury](https://home.treasury.gov/news) - fiscal policy and sanctions updates.
- [SEC](https://www.sec.gov/newsroom) - enforcement and market-structure rules.
- [Federal Reserve](https://www.federalreserve.gov/newsevents.htm) - rates, minutes, and monetary policy.
- [BLS](https://www.bls.gov/news.release/) - jobs and inflation releases.
- [U.S. Census Bureau](https://www.census.gov/newsroom.html) - population and economic indicators.
- [GAO](https://www.gao.gov/reports-testimonies) - audits and oversight reports.
- [CRS Reports](https://crsreports.congress.gov/) - nonpartisan congressional research.

### U.S. State-Level Starters
- [National Conference of State Legislatures](https://www.ncsl.org/) - 50-state legislative tracking.
- [National Governors Association](https://www.nga.org/) - state executive actions and policy initiatives.
- [USA.gov State Governments](https://www.usa.gov/state-governor) - direct state portal index.

### International Institutions
- [United Nations](https://news.un.org/en/) - global diplomatic and humanitarian updates.
- [IMF](https://www.imf.org/en/News) - macro-financial surveillance and outlooks.
- [World Bank](https://www.worldbank.org/en/news) - development and infrastructure policy.
- [OECD](https://www.oecd.org/newsroom/) - economic and social policy benchmarking.
- [WTO](https://www.wto.org/english/news_e/news_e.htm) - trade disputes and agreements.
- [European Central Bank](https://www.ecb.europa.eu/press/html/index.en.html) - eurozone policy signals.
- [Bank for International Settlements](https://www.bis.org/press/index.htm) - central bank and systemic-risk analysis.
